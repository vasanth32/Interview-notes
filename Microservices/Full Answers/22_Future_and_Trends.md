## 428. What is the difference between serverless microservices and traditional microservices?

**Serverless Microservices:**
- **Deployment**: FaaS
- **Scaling**: Automatic
- **Cost**: Pay per use
- **Management**: Platform-managed

**Traditional Microservices:**
- **Deployment**: Containers
- **Scaling**: Manual/auto
- **Cost**: Pay for running
- **Management**: Self-managed

**Comparison:**

| Aspect | Serverless | Traditional |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| **Deployment** | FaaS | Containers |
| **Scaling** | Automatic | Manual/auto |
| **Cost** | Pay per use | Pay for running |

**Best Practices:**
- Serverless for event-driven
- Traditional for long-running
- Choose based on needs
- Can combine both
